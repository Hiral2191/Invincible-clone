About

Saputara hill station is situated in the district of Dang. It is a part of the Sahyadri range of mountains and the highest point is at an altitude of 1081 metres from sea level. After the very first rain of the season, the whole area comes out full of greenery, frequent waterfalls and gushing streams. Saputara Adventure Camp is all about feeling the thrill of camping in these most beautiful hills......
